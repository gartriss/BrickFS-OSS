1.Patch Services.jar, Framework.jar, MIUI Services.jar, MIUI Framework.jar on this website: https://jefino9488.github.io/FrameworkPatcher/

services.jar and framework.jar are in system/system/framework
miui-services.jar and miui-framework.jar are in system_ext/framework

After patching, replace files

2.Debloat ROM (Remove all trash apps by Xiaomi). Be careful, if you will delete something wrong, system can bootloop!

3.Add this strings into product/etc/build.prop

persist.sys.background_blur_supported=true      |
persist.sys.background_blur_status_default=true | - Don't add this if your device is Low-End!
persist.sys.background_blur_version=2           |

ro.miui.product.home=com.miui.home

4.Add these strings to system/build.prop

ro.miui.ui.fonttype=mipro
ro.miui.ui.font.theme_apply=true
ro.miui.ui.font.animation=disable
ro.miui.ui.font.version=2
ro.miui.ui.font.mi_font_path=/system/fonts/MiSansVF.ttf
